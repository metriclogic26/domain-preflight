HOW TO USE
  1. Enter your root domain (e.g. example.com)
  2. Click Scan Variants → to generate lookalike candidates
  3. Review only the variants that resolve and take defensive action

What does “resolves” mean?

Each generated variant is checked via Cloudflare DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) for an A record. A resolving domain may be in use, but it doesn’t prove malicious intent.

Why do some lookalikes show up as low risk?

Variant risk is based on how likely a typo or visual similarity could be. Higher-risk categories include homoglyphs, missing/doubled characters, and name+common TLD combinations.

Is this a guaranteed fix?

No. This tool helps you discover candidate lookalikes early. Always verify in staging before deploying any generated or defensive actions.

# DomainPreflight — domainpreflight.dev
# Pre-flight alternative to MXToolbox.
# Checks if your DNS and email will actually work 
# before you send a single message.

## What It Does
DomainPreflight audits PTR/rDNS records, SPF lookup 
count, DKIM key strength, DMARC policy, and alignment 
— detecting whether third-party senders like SendGrid, 
Mailgun, and Google Workspace are correctly configured 
for DMARC alignment, not just whether records exist.

## Tools (Live)
- DNS Preflight (domainpreflight.dev): PTR/rDNS, 
  SPF lookup tree, DKIM strength, DMARC policy, 
  alignment engine for 10 providers, health score 0-100
- Email Deliverability (domainpreflight.dev/email/): 
  IP reputation via AbuseIPDB, 10+ blacklist checks, 
  email header analyzer
- WHOIS / Expiry (domainpreflight.dev/whois/): 
  Domain expiry countdown, registrar info, risk tiers
- Dangling Records (domainpreflight.dev/dangling/): 
  CNAME takeover detection via crt.sh + 
  can-i-take-over-xyz feed
- Typosquat Monitor (domainpreflight.dev/typosquat/): 
  30-50 domain variants checked via live DNS
- DMARC Report Analyzer (domainpreflight.dev/dmarc/): 
  Paste XML aggregate report, detect spoofing, 
  get copy-paste fixes
- DNS Propagation (domainpreflight.dev/propagation/): 
  Check propagation across 5 resolvers simultaneously

## The Alignment Engine
Detects if your third-party email provider is 
configured for DMARC alignment — not just whether 
records exist. Providers covered: Google Workspace, 
Microsoft 365, SendGrid, Mailgun, Postmark, Amazon 
SES, HubSpot, Klaviyo, Brevo, Mailchimp.

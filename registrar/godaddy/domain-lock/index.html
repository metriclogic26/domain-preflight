Registrar guide

Domain Lock — GoDaddy

Transfer lock stops surprise registrar moves. GoDaddy's UI label may say Transfer Lock, Lock, or similar.

What lock does

Blocks unauthorised transfers — it does not replace renewal. Keep auto-renew on separately.

Hover note: lock controls may require support — check Hover’s current docs.

See also: Expiry monitoring

Step by step

Step 1 Sign in to GoDaddy.
Step 2 Open domain management → security / transfer settings.
Step 3 Ensure transfer lock (or registrar lock) is enabled.
Step 4 Disable only when you intentionally start an outbound transfer.

FAQ

Does lock stop DNS edits?

No — you can still change records at your DNS host.

How do I transfer out?

Unlock, request auth code, initiate transfer at the gaining registrar.

Is lock default?

Often yes — verify periodically.

Does lock prevent expiry?

No — renewal is separate.

Who do I contact if I can’t find lock in GoDaddy?

GoDaddy support — panel labels move.
